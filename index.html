Top 4 movies of 2024:
THE SUBSTANCE is an outrageously crazy body-horror film that was one of the wildest movies I've ever seen. Not for the squeamish!
HERETIC is a psychological thriller that will have you questioning everything you believe in, while sitting on the edge of your seat.
IT'S WHAT'S INSIDE is a fun and simple movie about swapping bodies, and everything that could go wrong with abusing that power.
ODDITY is a creepy and insanely unsettling horror movie and cursed objects that will keep you awake at night.
